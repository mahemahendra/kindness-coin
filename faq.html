Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Kindness Coin Challenge

What is the Kindness Coin Challenge?

The Kindness Coin Challenge is a global initiative that encourages individuals to perform acts of kindness, share their stories, and pass the coin forward — creating a chain of compassion across communities.

Who can participate in the challenge?

While inspired by Lions and their spirit of service, the challenge is open to anyone who believes in the power of kindness and wishes to be part of this growing movement.

What do I do when I receive the coin?

The coin in your palm, has travelled a long distance. All you have to do is- fill out the form, document your story, and pass the coin forward to someone who will continue the journey.

Is the act of kindness required to be large or significant?

Not at all. Kindness is never measured by scale, but by sincerity. Even the smallest gesture can create a meaningful impact.

How are the stories shared?

Each story becomes part of a global digital Kindness Map, where every contribution adds to a collective narrative of compassion and service.

What is the purpose of this challenge?

To remind us that kindness connects us all — and that when shared, it has the power to inspire, uplift, and create lasting change across the world.

What's in it for me?

A chance to be part of something meaningful. To pause, to give, and to connect with others through kindness. While the act itself is selfless, the experience leaves behind a sense of purpose, fulfillment, and the quiet joy of knowing you made a difference — however small it may seem.
